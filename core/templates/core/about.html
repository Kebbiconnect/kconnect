About Kebbi Progressive Network

Strengthening youth mobilization and promoting good governance across Kebbi State

Our Constitution & Preamble

Our Vision

To build a progressive, transparent, and united Kebbi State where every citizen has equal opportunities for growth, development, and meaningful participation in governance and societal advancement.

Our Mission

The Kebbi Progressive Network exists to mobilize, empower, and unite the youth and citizens of Kebbi State through advocacy, education, welfare activities, and civic engagement to promote good governance, transparency, and sustainable development across all local government areas.

Our Aims & Objectives

Youth Mobilization

Mobilize and organize young people across all 21 LGAs in Kebbi State for positive civic engagement and community development.

Welfare Activities

Promote charity and welfare activities to support vulnerable communities and provide assistance to those in need.

Education & Empowerment

Empower citizens through educational initiatives, skill development programs, and economic empowerment opportunities.

Good Governance

Advocate for civic responsibility, transparency, accountability, and good governance at all levels of government.

Our Organizational Structure

State Executive

20 positions covering entire Kebbi State

Zonal Coordinators

3 zones with 3 coordinators each

LGA Leaders

21 LGAs with 10 leaders each

Ward Leaders

225 wards with 8 leaders each

Our Principles

  • Unity: One Voice, One Change - We believe in the power of collective action
  • Transparency: All our activities and decisions are open and accountable
  • Inclusivity: We welcome all citizens regardless of background or affiliation
  • Progress: We are committed to moving Kebbi State forward through innovation and development
  • Service: We exist to serve our communities and promote the common good
