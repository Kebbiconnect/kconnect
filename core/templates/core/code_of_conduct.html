Code of Conduct

Our Commitment

Kebbi Progressive Network is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all members.

Expected Behavior

  • Treat all members with respect and dignity
  • Promote peace, unity, and credible governance
  • Use social media responsibly and ethically
  • Support the organization's vision and mission

Prohibited Behavior

  • Hate speech or discrimination of any kind
  • Spreading fake news or misinformation
  • Political violence or incitement
  • Misuse of the group platform for personal gain
  • Harassment or bullying of members

Consequences

Violations of this code of conduct may result in:

  • Warning
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent dismissal from the organization

By joining KPN, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.
